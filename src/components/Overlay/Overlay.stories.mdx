# Overlay
A simple overlay component that is rendered over the top of all of the other elements on the page.
 
## Base
This is the element used in its simplest form.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Base">
    <div>
      <Overlay />
      <Text>I'm underneath the overlay</Text>
    </div>
  </Story>
</Preview>

## Variants
Pass a value to the `variant` prop to render the desired overlay.

### Inverse
<Preview>
  <Story name="Inverse" decorators={[inverseDecorator]}>
    <div>
      <Overlay variant="Inverse" />
      <Text>I'm underneath the overlay</Text>
    </div>
  </Story>
</Preview>

## Fixed
Pass the `fixed` prop to render the desired overlay.

`false` is the default value.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Fixed">
    <div>
      <Overlay fixed />
      <Text>I'm underneath the overlay</Text>
    </div>
  </Story>
</Preview>

## Content
Wrap children in the overlay component to render them inside the overlay.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Content">
    <div>
      <Overlay variant="Inverse">
        <Text className="m--t-md">I'm inside the overlay</Text>
      </Overlay>
      <Text>I'm underneath the overlay</Text>
    </div>
  </Story>
</Preview>
